Server Rules — read before joining
Admin  ## Rules ##

Breaking any of these rules will result in a warning, kick, or permanent ban depending on severity.

>ignorance is not a valid excuse
>staff decisions are final
>ban appeals go in discord

General Rules
  • §1 Be respectful to all players and staff. No harassment, slurs, or targeted toxicity.
  • §2 No griefing. Do not destroy, steal, or alter other players' builds without permission. BAN
  • §3 No hacking / cheating. X-ray, fly hacks, kill-aura, or any client modifications that give unfair advantages are banned. PERM BAN
  • §4 Keep chat in English or Turkish. No excessive spam, caps, or repeated messages.
  • §5 No advertising other servers in chat.
  • §6 No duplication glitches, exploiting bugs, or abusing mechanics that break the economy. Report them instead. BAN
  • §7 No excessive AFK farming that causes server lag.
  • §8 Do not build within 200 blocks of another player's build without permission.
PvP Rules
  • §P1 No spawn killing or trapping players at spawn.
  • §P2 No trapping or killing new players who haven't set a home yet. WARN → BAN
Punishment Scale
Admin

First offence: Verbal warning or 1-day ban.

Second offence: 7-day ban.

Third offence: Permanent ban.

Severe violations (hacking, doxxing, CSAM) = instant permanent ban, no appeal.

Appeal a ban at Discord.
